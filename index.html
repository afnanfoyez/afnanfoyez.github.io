Hello, My name is Afnan Foyez I am from Queens NY, currently studying computer science here at the University at Buffalo. My first semester here has been nothing short of amazing. The people here are extremely kind and always full of love and support. Coming from a city like Queens to one like Buffalo was a hard transition at first but as soon as classes began and I met like midned people it was all better. CSE 119 has been a surprising class for me, the flipped classroom method allowed us to be able to do hands on activities instead of listening to lectures. It was a different experice, one that helped me in other classes such as CS 115. The concents in the class were explained well and everything was understandable.
